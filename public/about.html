About the Author

Anthony Kim is a high school student at Phillips Academy Andover who is passionate about robotic engineering, 3D Computer-Aided Designing and Manufacturing, computer science, and making.

About the Research

The research process for this project was formed of visiting informative websites to collect data and organizing the data for effective data visualization. The purpose of this research was to find a correlation between the general trends found in the evolution process of animals and robots. Thus, the research for both animals and robots was done for important stages in the evolution process. Consequently, the data visualization may not be fully accurate.
