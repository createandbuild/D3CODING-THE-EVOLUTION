Humans, Humanoids

I researched and compared various characteristics of humans throughout their evolution and humanoid robots throughout their development. Specifically, I looked at the changes in the size, cranial or facial features, body structure, and the evolution.

What's Next?

I will continue research and expand onto the website in order to present interesting research data in the best way possible. Through future research, I hope to discover intriguing relationships between the evolutions of fish and biomimetic fish robot, as well as the evolutions of snake and biomimetic snake robot.
